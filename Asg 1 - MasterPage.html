Welcome To Z Swim

Swim With Us

Welcome to Z Swimming Academy! We have been one of Singapore's leading swimming academies since 2015, and our main coaches have at least 15 years of experience in swim coaching all over Singapore. Our coaches might be old, but that does not affect our teaching materials to continue to improve and evolve! We combine the latest teaching techniques with dynamic learning styles to squeeze every drop of fun out of every day. Start embracing the wonders of swimming as young as 4 years old! Swim with us in our progressive program, and your young athlete will graduate from our swim family as an accomplished swimmer with skills that will last a lifetime.
