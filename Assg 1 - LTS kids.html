Why Learn To Swim

Learning to swim is an essential life skill for children, providing them with safety, confidence, and a love for water-based activities! Early exposure to swimming equips kids with vital skills to prevent drowning and also enhances their physical development, coordination, and overall fitness.

At Z Swimming Academy, our learn-to-swim program introduces kids as young as 4 years old to the fundamentals of swimming in a fun and supportive environment. With our learn-to-swim progressive program alongside our experienced coachesâ€™ dynamic teaching techniques, kids will learn essential skills such as floating, kicking, basic strokes such as freestyle and breaststroke and finally, water trekking. As the students develop crucial swimming skills, they will also gain more confidence and a sense of achievement. The ultimate goal is always to make swimming fun for everyone!

So... Where do you go after Learn To Swim?

Development

In the Development Team, we will be refining student swimming techniques and teaching more advanced skills and surface training to ensure that the students remember every stroke by heart.

Intermediate

In the Intermediate Team, we will be teaching the students how to train further making sure that their techniques and skill basics are strong. Increasing stamina in swimming breaking their limits.

Advanced

In the Advanced Team, this place is where most of our competitive swimmers are. At this stage, the swimmers are not only instilled with goal setting skills, but also essential life skills that are developed through competitive swimming.
